E-commerce

Streamline your B2B sales process with WhatsApp-integrated e-commerce solutions that connect directly to your ERP system.

Key Features:
  • WhatsApp-first ordering: Customers request quotes and place orders in WhatsApp
  • Quotations & files: Auto-generate branded PDF/Excel quotes
  • Stock validation: Check availability in your ERP before taking payment
  • Secure payments: Hosted checkout links (Iyzico, Network International, etc.) with automatic success/failure handling
  • ERP integration: Push confirmed orders into your ERP such as Odoo, Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, etc.
  • Multi-tenant: Built for distributors and brands
How it works:
  • 1Customer requests a quote on WhatsApp
  • 2System returns quote + stock status
  • 3Customer taps a secure payment link
  • 4Order posts to ERP and confirmation is sent on WhatsApp & email
